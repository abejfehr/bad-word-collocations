# Bad Word Collocations

## In sequence

These are two words joined by and where in "A and B", "B" follows "A" in time, which is why it's weird to see these in the wrong order.

- error and trial
- dry and cut
- effect and cause

## Other conjunctions

These are words joined that must be in a certain order for cultural reasons.

- order and law
- pepper and salt
- dad and mom
- giggles and shits
- potatoes and meat
- bolts and nuts
- salad and soup
- ready and hot
- that and this
- robbers and cops
- butter and bread
- chips and fish
- downs and ups
- valleys and peaks
